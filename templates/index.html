About the Project

Objective

This website's main objective is to show and explain in a visual way the importance of analyzing, processing and validating data using Artificial Intelligence in order to help companies prevent possible reasons for dismissal.

Methodology

Our methodology involved preprocessing the data, training multiple machine learning models, and evaluating their performance. We conducted testing on both back-end and front-end components, ensuring functionality and system integration through automated tools.

Preprocessing

Data preprocessing transforms raw data into structured, useful formats. It is essential for analysis and modeling, ensuring relevant data selection and reliable results. Without a properly prepared Dataset, analysis and modeling results may be inaccurate or invalid. Therefore, carrying out adequate pre-processing is essential to ensure the quality and reliability of the conclusions obtained.

About the Datasets

Features

The selected attributes were JoiningYear, PaymentTier, Age, EverBenched, ExperienceInCurrentDomain, LeaveOrNot, Female, Male, Bachelors, Masters, and PHD.

Information

The dataset, with 1810 instances, is vital for understanding employee turnover. It offers insights into factors influencing retention, helping organizations make informed decisions to improve workforce stability.

Preprocessing

The preprocessing involved cleaning and normalizing the data, ensuring it was ready for model training. Categorical features were encoded, and the dataset was split into training and testing sets to evaluate model performance effectively. This step ensured the data's quality and consistency for accurate predictions.
